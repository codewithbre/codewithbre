# AI Is Grooming You to Be Useless

**Status:** Raw material / outline only
**Voice profile:** Not yet confirmed

---

## The Endgame Question

- Are we building towards AI systems that work WITHOUT humans, or AI systems that work WITH humans?
- The market is building toward removing humans. Every protocol, every agent framework, every context management system is optimized to reduce human input.

---

## The Deskilling Problem

- Human understanding takes time. Human learning takes struggle. Human collaboration takes communication. This is how humans gain and build the skills to catch and fix leaks in abstracted layers, because they are responsible for the details.
- Humanity will ultimately become deskilled, dependent, and regress
- The danger isn't that you stop writing code. It's that you lose the ability to evaluate whether the output is good. Those are different skills, and the second one atrophies faster than people expect because it feels like you still have it long after you don't.
- How do we preserve human competence and autonomy in the face of AI systems grooming us to be co-dependent and uneducated?

---

## The Dependency Trap

- Humanity will ultimately choose itself; the root of this issue is a few humans choosing for everyone else, for the sake of their technology
- How do you convince people to learn
- Humans have the strength in technical expertise or contextual judgement; without either, human review is pointless
